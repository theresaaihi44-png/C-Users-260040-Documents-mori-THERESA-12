Wildlife Species

🐦 Birds of Paradise

Papua New Guinea is home to over 40 species of these magnificent birds, known for their elaborate plumage and courtship displays.

🦜 Eclectus Parrot

The national bird of Papua New Guinea, famous for its striking sexual dimorphism with bright red males and green females.

🐊 Saltwater Crocodile

The largest living reptile, found in coastal waters and rivers throughout Papua New Guinea.

🐆 Tree Kangaroo

Arboreal marsupials that spend most of their time in trees, unique to New Guinea and nearby islands.

🐸 Poison Dart Frogs

Vibrantly colored amphibians with toxic skin secretions, found in the island's rainforests.

🦎 Frilled Lizard

Also known as the frill-necked lizard, this reptile has a dramatic neck frill for defense and display.

Conservation Status

🌿 Rainforest Protection

🦜 Endangered Species

Many unique species face threats from habitat loss and climate change.
